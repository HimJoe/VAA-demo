Navigating Integration: Challenges & Strategy

Key Challenges

  • Data readiness and quality assurance
  • Integration with legacy systems
  • Ensuring ethical use (bias, fairness, transparency)
  • Building user trust and managing change
  • Defining appropriate levels of autonomy and oversight
  • Developing new skills and adapting roles

The Strategic Approach (SVAI Framework)

Successful integration requires a structured approach like the Strategic VAA Integration (SVAI) Framework, emphasizing:

  • Strategic alignment with business goals
  • Iterative process redesign with the VAA in mind
  • Rigorous pilot testing and validation
  • Proactive ethical governance ("Ethics by Design")
  • Continuous monitoring, optimization, and adaptation
